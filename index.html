Dan Garrette

I am a Research Scientist at Google Research
in New York. My research focuses on Natural
Language Processing and Machine Learning.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at
The University of Texas at Austin in 2015 and
was later a postdoc in Computer Science at
the University of Washington.
